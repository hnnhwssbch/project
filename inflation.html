The dataset we use stems from the Bank of England Research datasets.

I quote:

This dataset contains the individual responses to our Inflation Attitudes Survey, a quarterly survey of people’s feelings about inflation and other economic variables like the interest rate.

For this dataset the Bank of England asked 240.133 people for their opinion on the perceived and expected inflation. The survey has run quarterly since 2001.
